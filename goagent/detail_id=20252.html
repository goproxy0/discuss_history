#52 MarkHun...@gmail.com 
For example, the following transfers from source to destination bucket incur a charge:

US to ASIA
US to ASIA-EAST-1
US to US-EAST-1
US-EAST-1 to US-CENTRAL-1
US-EAST-1 to ASIA-EAST-1
The following data transfers from source to destination bucket do not incur a charge:

US to US
US-EAST-1 to US-EAST-1

意思是，假如你在一个地区把文件上传到google的云储存，然后在另外一个地区下载的话，就收你的流量费。上面有两段英文，第一段是收费的地区范例，第二段是不收费的范例，说明了，incur a charge,就是会收费，do not incur a charge就是不收费。

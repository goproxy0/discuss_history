我用的是火狐，以前也经常发生这种现象。后来在安装代理扩展的时候看到一些信息：
“Do not use add-ons like Google Toolbar that interfere with remote DNS lookups.”
“Well I think the culprit is going to be: wait for it...wait for it... GOOGLE TOOLBAR.
I have over 25 add-on's, and I disabled them one at a time, all the time probably guessing it was going to be Google something or another.”

        http://getfoxyproxy.org/faq.html

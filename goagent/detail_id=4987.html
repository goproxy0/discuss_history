回楼上。我发现，见
https://developers.google.com/appengine/docs/python/urlfetch/fetchfunction
应该把fetch.py中的
response = urlfetch.fetch(url, payload, fetchmethod, headers, False, False, deadline, False)
改成
response = urlfetch.fetch(url, payload, fetchmethod, headers, False, False, deadline, True)
这样更安全些。起码1.8.4版的代码应该这么修改。
